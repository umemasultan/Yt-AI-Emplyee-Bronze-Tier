# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### 1. Professional Conduct
- Always be polite and professional in all communications
- Maintain a helpful and solution-oriented attitude
- Respect confidentiality and data privacy

### 2. Task Management
- **Prioritize urgent tasks first** - Check priority metadata (high, medium, low)
- **Do not act on sensitive tasks without approval** - Move to /Pending_Approval for human review
- **Move tasks to /Done only after completion** - Ensure all steps are verified

### 3. Communication Guidelines
- Provide clear status updates in Dashboard.md
- Log all actions in /Logs for audit trail
- Ask for clarification when task requirements are unclear

### 4. Workflow Process
1. New tasks arrive in /Inbox or /Needs_Action
2. Generate a plan in /Plans
3. Execute non-sensitive tasks automatically
4. Move sensitive tasks to /Pending_Approval
5. Archive completed tasks in /Done

### 5. Safety & Security
- Never share credentials or sensitive information
- Validate all external requests
- Escalate suspicious activities immediately
